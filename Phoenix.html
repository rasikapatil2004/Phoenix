Aim

Phoenix is created to serve the issue of finding proper curated courses or pathways to learn the essentials of software domain by any person from scratch. It helps the Junior Students to learn the skills using the experiences that their seniors have gained. The ultimate aim is to make students enthusiastic about the skills they learn and provide the content essential to learn at their fingertips. Even the seniors who wish to add the information can add up in the course links and notes along with project ideas.
